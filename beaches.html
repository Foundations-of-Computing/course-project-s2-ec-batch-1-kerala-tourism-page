Beaches in Kerala


Kovalam Beach

One of the most popular beaches in Kerala, known for its crescent-shaped coastline, lighthouse, and water sports.

District: Thiruvananthapuram

Varkala Beach

Also known as Papanasam Beach, it offers stunning cliffs, mineral springs, and a spiritual retreat.

District: Thiruvananthapuram

Cherai Beach

A perfect blend of backwaters and sea, ideal for swimming and dolphin spotting.

District: Ernakulam

Marari Beach

A serene beach known for its golden sands and peaceful ambiance, perfect for relaxation.

District: Alappuzha

Bekal Beach

Famous for the historic Bekal Fort and breathtaking sunset views.

District: Kasargod

Muzhappilangad Beach

India’s longest drive-in beach, perfect for adventure lovers.

District: Kannur

Payyoli Beach

A tranquil beach ideal for swimming and turtle spotting.

District: Kozhikode

Shankumugham Beach

Famous for its giant mermaid sculpture and breathtaking sunsets.

District: Thiruvananthapuram

Poovar Beach

A pristine beach where the Neyyar River meets the Arabian Sea, offering boat rides and golden sands.

District: Thiruvananthapuram

Meenkunnu Beach

A hidden gem, offering solitude and stunning views of the Arabian Sea.

District: Kannur
