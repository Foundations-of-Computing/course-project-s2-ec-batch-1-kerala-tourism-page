Forts in Kerala


Bekal Fort

The largest fort in Kerala, known for its scenic coastal views and historic significance.

District: Kasargod

Anjengo Fort

A British-era fort with colonial history and stunning backwater views.

District: Thiruvananthapuram

Palakkad Fort

A well-preserved fort built by Hyder Ali, featuring a moat and lush gardens.

District: Palakkad

Thalassery Fort

A British-built fort overlooking the Arabian Sea, significant in colonial history.

District: Kannur

Hosdurg Fort

Known for its unique circular bastions and serene surroundings.

District: Kasargod

St. Angelo Fort

A Portuguese-built fort with a beautiful sea-facing location.

District: Kannur

Chandragiri Fort

A scenic riverside fort offering panoramic views of the Payaswini River.

District: Kasargod

Kollam Fort

An ancient trade hub with remnants of Portuguese and Dutch influences.

District: Kollam

Kodungallur Fort

Also known as Cranganore Fort, this structure reflects the region’s colonial past.

District: Thrissur

Poonjar Fort

A historic fort showcasing Kerala’s royal heritage and architecture.

District: Kottayam
