Famous Temples in Kerala


Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple

One of the richest temples in the world, dedicated to Lord Vishnu, known for its Dravidian-style architecture.

Location: Thiruvananthapuram

Guruvayur Temple

A famous Krishna temple, considered the Dwarka of the South, attracting thousands of devotees daily.

Location: Thrissur

Sabarimala Temple

Dedicated to Lord Ayyappa, this temple is one of the largest annual pilgrimage destinations in the world.

Location: Pathanamthitta

Vadakkunnathan Temple

An ancient Shiva temple, known for its grand Thrissur Pooram festival and mural paintings.

Location: Thrissur

Ettumanoor Mahadeva Temple

A Shiva temple famous for its stunning murals and traditional oil lamps.

Location: Kottayam

Chottanikkara Temple

Dedicated to Goddess Bhagavathy, this temple is believed to have miraculous healing powers.

Location: Ernakulam

Ambalapuzha Sree Krishna Temple

Famous for its delicious "Palpayasam" offering and Krishna idol resembling Guruvayurappan.

Location: Alappuzha

Thirunelli Temple

Dedicated to Lord Vishnu, this temple is situated in the Brahmagiri Hills and is a major pilgrimage center.

Location: Wayanad

Mannarasala Nagaraja Temple

A temple dedicated to serpent gods, attracting devotees seeking fertility and prosperity blessings.

Location: Alappuzha

Lokanarkavu Temple

A historically significant temple linked to the legendary warrior Thacholi Othenan.

Location: Kozhikode
