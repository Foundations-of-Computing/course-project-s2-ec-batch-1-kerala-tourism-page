Famous Hill Stations in Kerala


Munnar

Known for its tea plantations, misty hills, and breathtaking landscapes.

Location: Idukki

Wayanad

A serene hill station with lush forests, waterfalls, and wildlife sanctuaries.

Location: Wayanad

Ponmudi

A picturesque hill station with winding roads, tea gardens, and stunning viewpoints.

Location: Thiruvananthapuram

Nelliyampathy

A hidden gem known for its dense forests, orange plantations, and scenic valleys.

Location: Palakkad

Peermedu

A tranquil hill station famous for its spice plantations and cool climate.

Location: Idukki

Agasthyarkoodam

A trekking paradise with rich biodiversity and spiritual significance.

Location: Thiruvananthapuram
