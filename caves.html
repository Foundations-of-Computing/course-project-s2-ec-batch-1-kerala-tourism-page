Famous Caves in Kerala


Edakkal Caves

Ancient caves with petroglyphs dating back to the Neolithic Age, offering a glimpse into prehistoric life.

Location: Wayanad

Kottukal Cave Temple

A rock-cut temple with beautiful carvings dedicated to Lord Shiva and Hanuman.

Location: Kollam

Pandavan Para

Believed to be the resting place of the Pandavas from Mahabharata, this cave is a historical marvel.

Location: Pathanamthitta

Ancode Rock Shelter

Prehistoric rock shelters with unique carvings and natural formations.

Location: Thiruvananthapuram

Ponmudi Caves

A hidden gem in the Western Ghats, these caves are surrounded by lush greenery and serene landscapes.

Location: Thiruvananthapuram

Kurisumala Caves

A religious site and pilgrimage spot known for its serene environment and historical significance.

Location: Kottayam
